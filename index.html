Who are we?

Fix'n Right is all about the right to repair! This page is dedicated to providing info about the right to repair movement, and what it takes to keep your devices repaired and in working condition. On this site you'll find various repair manuals as well as information about the right to repair movement and an interactive page to learn about how phones work.
